Hi there! Are you an avid reader who can never get enough of books? You've come to the right place! BookWorm lets you lend books to your friends and earn points in return. Using your points, you can request books from other users.

Start reading in a few simple steps:

1. Select the book you want to read.

2. Request the book from the nearest lender.

3. Wait for your book to arrive, read and mail it back to the sender.



If you're more of the giving type,

1. Add the books you want to lend to your Book shelf.

2. Accept incoming book requests from borrowers and earn points.

3. Mail it to the borrower.
